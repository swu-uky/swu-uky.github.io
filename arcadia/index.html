The path shown here is a common route I would take to and from school (specifically during the middle school years). It takes a little less than an hour in its entirety and passes by significant landmarks such as the Westfield super mall (more than 1 million sq ft) and the Santa Anita Race Track, host to some of the more prominent horse races every year!
