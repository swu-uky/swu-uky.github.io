California Waterways

Authored by: Shan Huan Wu

The map above details the streams and waterbodies in the state of California. Major waterbodies are labeled if the area exceeds 50 square miles. It was made possible through QGIS 3.8 with data acquired from The National Map. Background image of Lake Tahoe taken from Lake Tahoe Balloons.
